# Topic 1 - Introduction to Business Analytics

This topic covers the key conceptual aspects of business analytics. The contents in this topic are aimed to help you understand the fundamental concepts of business analytics, its development, the roles of business analysts, and the ethical considerations in business analytics.

- **Topic 1.1 - Concepts of Business Analytics**: This section introduces the basic concepts and definitions related to business analytics, including what it is, its components, and how it differs from related fields like data science and business intelligence.
- **Topic 1.2 - Development of Business Analytics**: This section traces the historical development of business analytics, highlighting key milestones and how the field has evolved over time.
- **Topic 1.3 - The Roles of Business Analysts**: This section explores the various roles and responsibilities of business analysts in organizations, including the skills and competencies required for success in this field.
- **Topic 1.4 - Ethics of Business Analytics**: This section discusses the ethical considerations that business analysts must take into account when working with data, including issues related to bias, privacy, and fairness.
